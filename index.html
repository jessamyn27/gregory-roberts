    Gregory Roberts Creative Profile

    Gregory Roberts is a musician, songwriter and founding member of the NYC-based band White Rabbits. Roberts has also worked with acclaimed artists like Britt Daniel of Spoon and Rostam Batmanglij of Vampire Weekend, and has shared the stage with acts such as Kevin Morby, Arcade Fire, The National and Muse. He is currently serving as long-standing bassist and backing vocalist for the beloved singer-songwriter Hamilton Leithauser (The Walkmen), recently wrapping up another successful month long residency at the Cafe Carlyle in New York City.
